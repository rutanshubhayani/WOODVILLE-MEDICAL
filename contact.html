Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

Are your products FDA approved?

Yes, all our medical equipment and supplies are FDA approved and meet the highest safety and quality standards.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging. Medical consumables are non-returnable for safety reasons.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer competitive bulk pricing for healthcare facilities, institutions, and large quantity orders. Contact our sales team for custom quotes.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 days) and same-day delivery are available in select areas.
